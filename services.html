Our Services

We deliver expert 2D/3D Design drafting, process design, and technical consulting services helping you move from concept to completion with confidence.

Request a Quote About Us

3D Modeling & Design

We create detailed, production ready 3D models from individual components to complex multi-part assemblies. Our models are built for clarity, accuracy, and seamless integration into your product development, prototyping and production workflows.

  • Part and assembly modeling
  • Sheet metal and weldment design
  • Photorealistic rendering and visualization
  • Design for manufacturability (DFM) review
  • BOM generation and revision management

AutoCAD Drawings & Drafting

Our drafting team produces accurate 2D drawings in AutoCAD. Whether you need architectural layouts, mechanical details, or general arrangement drawings, we deliver clean, well organized documentation.

  • Mechanical and structural detail drawings
  • General arrangement (GA) drawings
  • Fabrication and shop drawings
  • As-built documentation and redline markups
  • Drawing conversion (paper-to-CAD)

Process Design

We develop robust process designs that form the backbone of your operations. Our deliverables include process flow diagrams, P&IDs, heat and mass balances, and supporting calculations, all aligned with industry standards.

  • Process flow diagrams (PFDs)
  • Piping and instrumentation diagrams (P&IDs)
  • Heat and mass balance development
  • Process simulation and optimization
  • Hazard and operability (HAZOP) support

Piping Design

Our piping design services cover the full lifecycle, from routing studies and stress considerations through to detailed isometric drawings. We focus on constructability, operability, and maintainability in every layout.

  • Piping layout and routing studies
  • Isometric drawings and spool generation
  • Pipe support design and placement
  • Material take-offs (MTOs)
  • Compliance with ASME, CSA, and industry codes

Equipment Selection

We help you identify and specify the right equipment for your process backed by technical evaluation, vendor comparison, and lifecycle cost analysis. Our selections are driven by performance data, not guesswork.

  • Pumps, compressors, and blowers
  • Heat exchangers and cooling systems
  • Pressure vessels and storage tanks
  • Vendor bid evaluation and comparison
  • Technical data sheet preparation
